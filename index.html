Finding Number of Cubes that can be formed

Cubes of dimension 2x2x2 can be formed using 4 Black and 4 White 1x1x1 cubes in 7 different ways.

If we divide the cube into 2 planes, we can arrange blocks in each plane in following ways.

1. All 4 Black Cubes in one plane

2. 3 Black Cubes and 1 White Cube in one plane

1. All 4 Black Cubes in one plane
